Back Yard Burgers

Fri 01 September 2006

After reading the glowing review of Back Yard Burgers over at Metroblogging Dallas, I finally got around to trying out Back Yard Burgers last night. I'm sorry that I have to be the one to say this, but they really don't live up to the name. Burgers cooked in my back yard are juicy and never overcooked. The bacon cheddar burger I tried was well done and dry. The bacon was overcooked, too. I didn't really taste much of the cheddar, but the onions and pickles masked the tomato quite well. What was missing? Oh yeah, lettuce—it was so small I didn't even taste it. I think YUM could make a better burger. ;)
